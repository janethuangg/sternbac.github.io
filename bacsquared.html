About The Conference
The Business Analytics Club Business Analytics Conference is BACs premire event to further develop students’ understanding of analytics work and the underlying industry attached to it. This year we will be hostinga number of companies who are leaders in the analytics world, including PwC, Spotify, and AppNexus. The event consists of a Keynote presentation about what it means to work in analytics and what competencies, outside of pure coding, are required for students to be successful in the industry. The event will also include a meet & greet with the companies, a corporate panel where representitives from the companies will be able to share their insight and experience, followed by a networking reception with refreshments. We are very excited for this event and look forward to seeing all you business analytics gurus there!
