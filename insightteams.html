About The Insight Team
The Insight Team Team is a new initiative launched by the undergraduate Business Analytics Club. The primary goal is to build upon the technical skills of students through workshops and participation in a semester-long data science project. We hope for the program to foster a sense of community for Stern students interested in the fields of "tech" and "analytics" as well as provide industry insight so that they can learn about career opportunities with firms that do not traditionally recruit at Stern.
